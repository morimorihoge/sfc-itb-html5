Mid-term Assignment : Make a Simple Self-Introduction Web Page

Make a simple self-introduction web page, based on the skills and techniques we have gone through. (Refer to your instructor for detailed instructions)

Details

You must design your web page by CSS. You can refer HTML5 reference or find many other web pages if you search.

Conditions

Make sure you fullfil the following requirements upon completing your assignment.

  • Attach at least one image to your site.
  • Define at least one hyperlink, and make sure it jumps to the destination properly.
  • Make sure your introduction contains materials equivalent to one page in A4 size. The contents can be anything from basic information to a list of hobbies.
  • Refer to your instructor for any other conditions.

Submission Method and Deadline

Publish your web site under http://web.sfc.keio.ac.jp/~[CNS Account Name]/itb_mid/. Make sure it is accessable via the internet using a browser. If this URL was to be inaccessible at the time of the deadline, your assignment would be considered invalid.

Refer to your instructor for any other conditions.
